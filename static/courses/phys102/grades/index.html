Your final grades have been submitted to the faculty so you can check them with the Student Service Centre but I am also posting them here for convenience. The class average was 73% which is slightly above previous years.

I won't post marks for the separate components because (I've been told) that just invites complaints and there's no point: I can't make any changes anyway. If you want to know your mark breakdown, let me know and I'll email it to you. Keep in mind the average mark for the final exam was 64% so that might make your overall grade somewhat lower than you were hoping.

You were a great set of students and I really enjoyed working with you all. Best of luck in the future! I hope to see you around campus.

Rik
